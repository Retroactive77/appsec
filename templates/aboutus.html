WHAT WE DO


At Karang Guni Pte Ltd, we aim to help the environment by recycling and reusing items that would otherwise be thrown away. We also aim to help the community by providing jobs to those who need it, and by providing a platform for people to sell their items.

We have pledged 5% of our total sales towards conservations of the Earth, as well as helping those in third-world countries by donating to reputable charities. Join us today on our journey to save and protect the Earth!

Recently, I have started thrifting and I found that thrifting clothes actually make me much happier! I've found a lot of clothes that actually suit my style, and I encourage others to start thrifting as well! Who knows what you will find?

Eugene Franster

My family and I have recently started doing an earth hour of our own everyday, turning off all electronics and lights at certain times of the day to save energy. We use that hour to bond, making us closer than before.
