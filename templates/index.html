WHO ARE WE?

Established in 2023, Karang Guni Pte Ltd is a small business that aims to play our part in saving the environment. We provide the public with a platform to not only thrift clothes online but learn more about the environment too.

We have also pledged 5% of our sales to conservation of the environment.

WHY SHOP
SECOND HAND?

Purchasing second-hand clothes is not only cost saving, it reduces waste too. 18.6 trillion tonnes of clothing are sent to landfills or incinerated every year. Buying second-hand clothing prolongs the lifespan of these garments.

Do visit our blog to learn more about environmental practices here!
